## 📖 About Ghar Ki Rasoee

**Ghar Ki Rasoee** is a premium tiffin service dedicated to delivering fresh, healthy, and hygienic home-style meals directly to your doorstep. We believe that food is not just fuel; it's an emotion, a connection to home, and a source of comfort.

Ghar Ki Rasoee's mission is simple: To provide **"Ghar Ka Khana"** (Home Food) away from home, ensuring you never have to compromise on taste or health.

## 🍽️ Ghar Ki Rasoee Offerings

We have crafted three distinct plans to suit your appetite:

### 1. The Basic Tiffin

_Perfect for light eaters or a quick, healthy lunch._

- **Includes**: 4 Roti, Seasonal Sabzi, Salad.

### 2. The Standard Thali

_A perfectly balanced meal for a satisfying lunch or dinner._

- **Includes**: 4 Roti, Seasonal Sabzi, Dal, Rice, Salad, Pickle.

### 3. The Premium Feast

_For those who want it all—a complete dining experience._

- **Includes**: 4 Roti, 2 Sabzis, Dal Makhani/Special Dal, Pulao/Jeera Rice, Sweet, Papad, Salad, Pickle.
